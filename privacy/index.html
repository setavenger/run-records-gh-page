Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

RunRecords respects your privacy. This app is designed to process data on your device and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

Health Data

With your permission, RunRecords may read selected health and fitness data from Apple Health / HealthKit, including:

  • Workout sessions (currently running workouts)
  • Heart rate
  • Running speed
  • GPS workout routes

This data is used only to calculate and display statistics inside the app — charts, trends, splits, maps, and related workout analytics. Health data is processed on your device. It is not sent to our servers, sold, shared with third parties, used for advertising, or used for marketing.

You can manage or revoke Health permissions at any time in the Apple Health app or iOS Settings.

Data Collection

RunRecords does not collect personal data, analytics data, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, location data, or health data off your device.

Data Storage

Imported workouts, computed metrics, and app preferences are stored locally on your device in the app’s sandboxed storage. You can delete this data by using Clear All Data in Settings, or by deleting the app from your device.

Third Parties

RunRecords does not share user data with third parties and does not include advertising or tracking services.
